### 1. Purpose
Defines required evidence artifacts to support audits, incident reviews,
and compliance verification.

### 2. Evidence Categories

#### 2.1 Planning Evidence
- PRD documents
- Acceptance criteria
- Test and deployment plans

#### 2.2 CI Evidence
- CI logs and results
- Security scan outputs
- Dependency audit reports

#### 2.3 Deployment Evidence
- Staging deployment logs
- Production deployment logs
- Verification and rollback records

#### 2.4 Learning Evidence
- Post-run reports
- Lessons learned
- Root cause analyses

### 3. Evidence Requirements
- Evidence must be immutable once generated
- Evidence must be timestamped
- Evidence must be traceable to PR, run ID, and milestone

### 4. Retention Policy
- Evidence must be retained according to organizational policy
- Deletion or modification of evidence is prohibited

### 5. Audit Usage
Evidence must be sufficient for:
- Internal audits
- External audits
- Incident and post-mortem analysis
